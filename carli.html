Meet the Stylist

Carli Martin

Hello, I'm a Cosmetologist in the Plymouth, Minnesota area. I began my cosmetology journey by doing my own hair as well as my friends- the usual fun hair colors and coloring extensions. I started up a shop on the app Depop selling custom colored extensions and wigs that I handmade. I colored and cut the wigs myself. Upon finishing high school I enrolled and developed my education from PCI Academy.
I do makeup as well from time to time. I have a great passion for this and enjoy the many ways I can be creative.
"Love of beauty is taste, the creation of beauty is art."
